Max Garfin

About

Max Garfin is an American actor born and raised in Manhattan, New York. A proud alumnus of SUNY Purchase BFA Acting and LaGuardia High School's Drama Program, Max has built an impressive career spanning film and television. He is also the founder of Day Of Productions, a New York-based production company dedicated to telling compelling stories. His recent high-profile work includes starring roles in The Four Seasons and You Can't Run Forever.
